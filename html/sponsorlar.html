WHY WE NEED SUPPORTERS

As AMAL HAWKS, the financial burdens on us are actually much greater than they appear. We are in need of financial resources for the FRC Regional event that we plan to attend in 2024 with a team of 20 people, and we aim to stay for 12 days.

OUR MAIN SPONSORS

One of the most important sponsors of our team is 3M. They support us since 2022 by giving us grants, and currently, there is a mentor from 3M in our team. We as Team 7611 thank 3M a lot because of the support that they supply to our team.

Participating in our first FRC competition was almost an impossible dream for us, but thanks to the presentation by our alumni member Berk, we were able to obtain our plane tickets and make history at the Long Island Regional, all thanks to Ford.

One of our most important sponsors is, of course, Turkish Cargo. They have been supporting us since our first FRC Regional. We are extremely proud to have such an important and valuable company from our country represented on our uniforms and posters.

Of course, we cannot forget our school, which supports us as much as possible in our work at the workshop and whenever we need it. We thank our school, which opens its doors for us morning and evening and supports us wherever we go and whatever we do
