WHO ARE WE?

As the Amal Hawks Robotics Team, we are a robotics team formed by students and teacher Ali Kemal Alemdaroğlu at Bahçelievler Adnan Menderes Anatolian High School in Turkey in 2017. We strive to have an impact on our country and the world through our ongoing projects. Our team which was established in 2017, currently consists of 6 female students, 17 male students, and 2 male and 1 female mentors (3M). Our initial thought when starting our work was to prove that with sufficient support, high school students can become beneficial individuals for our future by working hard. We aimed to encourage these highly potential students to participate in science and technology projects at a young age, aligning with our country’s and the world’s needs. At this point, we believed that introducing young people to robots and providing them with opportunities to work in this field would bring out their potential and take them a step further. Accordingly, we moved in this direction and, as a result, we have carried out numerous projects, produced various products, gained experience, and achieved success. Each success we achieved motivated us to work harder and be more dedicated to our work.

If we need to give examples of some robots that we, as Amal Hawks 7611, have made; FRC robots, maze-solving robots, Caretta Caretta (Egg Collection) robots, line-following robots, Arduino projects, and terrain robots can be mentioned.
